[Title_en]
Midnight Elevator

[Surface_en]
A man returned to his apartment late at night and pressed floor 15 in the elevator.
As the doors closed, he suddenly shouted, "Great, I'm finally safe!"
The next day, he was found dead in the hallway on floor 15.

[Bottom_en]
He was being followed, and the killer was waiting on floor 15.
Another resident had been in the elevator with him, so he mistakenly felt safer while not alone.
That resident got off on floor 14, and then the killer murdered him on floor 15.

[Title_zh]
午夜电梯

[Surface_zh]
一个男人深夜回到公寓，在电梯里按了15楼。
电梯门关上时，他突然大喊："太好了，我终于安全了！"
第二天，人们发现他死在15楼的走廊里。

[Bottom_zh]
他被人跟踪了，凶手正在15楼等他。
电梯里还有另一位住户，他误以为有人陪着就安全了。
那位住户在14楼下电梯后，凶手在15楼杀害了他。
