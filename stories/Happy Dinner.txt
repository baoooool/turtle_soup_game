[Title]
Happy Dinner

[Surface]
A family is happily eating dinner. The father answers a phone call, turns pale and sweaty, and stares at his wife and son who are eating meat.

[Bottom]
Days earlier, the family was caught in an avalanche while climbing a snowy mountain, and the father was separated. He survived and came home to find his wife and son waiting with a meal, believing they had also returned safely. Then the rescue team called: "We just found your wife and son's bodies in a snow pit." So who are the people eating across from him?
