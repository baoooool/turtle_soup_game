[Title]
The Cat in the Attic

[Surface]
A woman who lives alone hears faint cat meows from the attic every night. One day she finally goes up to check, finds nothing, and after coming down she moves out that very night.

[Bottom]
She does not own a cat. The attic is a sealed, dusty room with no trace of an animal. She realizes the "meowing" was not a cat at all, but someone hiding in the wall cavity, playing a faint cat recording on a phone to cover their heavy breathing.
