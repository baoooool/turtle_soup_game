[Title]
The Last Bus

[Surface]
Late at night, a little girl boards the last bus. The bus is full of passengers, but at the next stop she suddenly bolts off and immediately calls the police.

[Bottom]
After boarding, she notices the bus is unnaturally quiet. Looking closely, she sees the passengers sitting rigidly with pale faces and empty eyes. Worst of all, each neck is tied to a nearly transparent fishing line leading to the ceiling. Everyone except her and the driver is a corpse from a recent murder, and the driver is puppeteering them.
