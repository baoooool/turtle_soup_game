[Title]
The Perfect Photo

[Surface]
A photographer captures a stunning photo of a volcanic eruption and wins an international award. On the eve of the ceremony, he kills himself in his studio.

[Bottom]
To get the perfect close-up, he entered a forbidden danger zone. During the eruption, a young couple in front of him begged for help. He chose not to help so he would not ruin the composition and timing, and instead pressed the shutter. After the award, their desperate faces haunted him, and he could not bear the guilt.
